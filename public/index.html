The Wonders of 3D Printing

Published by: Jordan Bryan

3-D printing is a beautiful hobby to pick up due to its multitude of applications. The technology has become cheap enough for the general consumer market and has revolutionized the industry. Social media website and other file sharing locations take advantage of the number of printers in commission today. These sites allow for ordering of printed parts as well as the collaboration that is highly beneficial for novices. You don't need to be a 3-D modeling pro to be able to get the practical use out of your printer. So many designs are shared for free online and anyone can modify or print these designs to suit their needs.

3-D Printing for Beginners:

While many look at 3-D printing as an alien technology the field can be broken into with a $200 investment. A great beginning 3-D printer is the Monoprice 3-D Select (Buy Here) The same printer is shown below with a 120x120x120mm heated print bed it provides enough space for small models, or modular parts for larger ones. The entry level printer even comes with Wi-Fi printing capabilities so you can print on the go. 3-D Printing for Experienced Users:

While the Monoprice is an amazing start for a beginner the Creality CR-10 (Buy Here) is an unparalleled 3-D printer. With a 300x300x400mm heated pyrex printbed the printer when properly tuned can produce professional quality prints. As well as it allows for larger scale prints that increases the imagination of the user. The printers themselves are amazing due to the modifications that can be printed and applied to the printer to increase its productivity. The machine practically fixes itself (It doesn't. Please consult the proper repair documentation)
